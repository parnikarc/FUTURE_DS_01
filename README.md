## üß≠ Dashboard Pages

### 1. **Business Sales Overview**

**Highlights:**
- Overall summary of sales performance and profitability.
- Trend line of monthly sales over time.
- Category-wise comparison of Sales vs Profit.
- Segment-based sales distribution (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).
- Interactive slicers for filtering by Year, Region, and Segment.

---

### 2. **Regional Performance Overview**
**Highlights:**
- Geographic map visualization of **Sales by State**.
- Top 10 states ranked by total sales (California, New York, etc.).
- **Profit Margin % by State** heatmap to identify strong and weak regions.
- Filters by Year, Region, and Segment.

---

### 3. **Category & Subcategory Insights**
**Highlights:**
- **Top 10 Products by Sales** bar chart.
- **Sales vs Profit by Sub-Category** scatter plot to assess profitability.
- **Sales vs Profit by Category & Segment** treemap for a detailed visual breakdown.
- Drill-down capabilities to explore underperforming categories.

---

### 4. **Segment & Ship Mode Analysis**

**Highlights:**
- **Total Sales & Profit by Segment** (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).
- **Sales and Profit by Ship Mode** (Standard Class, Second Class, First Class, Same Day).
- **Profit Margin % by Ship Mode** matrix table for detailed analysis.
- Helps evaluate logistics efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

---

## üöÄ Insights & Recommendations
- **Consumer segment** drives the highest profit (‚âà 134K).
- **Technology category** yields the most revenue and profit.
- **California** and **New York** lead in sales contribution.

- Focus improvement on **Office Supplies** and **Furniture** subcategories with low margins.
